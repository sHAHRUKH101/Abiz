Exit

Exit

An “exit strategy” is exactly that… “how to get out of the business”… it’s a little like walking on the moon... you have to be prepared!

When you are ready to sell your business or, pass it along to family members and/or partners, we’re here to assist. The window period may be very small. You need to be well prepared when the right potential buyer comes along.

DOCUMENTS YOU WILL NEED

Year #1, #2 and #5 Income / Expense Analysis & Projections
Year #1 and #5 Cash Flow
Best / Worse Case Analysis
Return On Investment (ROI)
SWOT (Strength, Weaknesses, Opportunities & Threats) Analysis
Profit & Loss Statements

This is just the beginning!
Then you need to ask: What are your reasons for selling?
Voluntary or involuntary?

If voluntary, before approaching a “broker” or “consultant”, you will need documentation:
Marketing Plan Executive Summary Tax Records Company History
If involuntary, are you being forced into it by death of a partner or other? You will need this documentation:
Buy-Sell Agreement Life Insurance Corporate Documentation / Succession Planning

THIS CAN BE OVERWHELMING! WHERE IS THE EXIT?

Don’t panic – we’re here to help. During our consultations, we will be covering several sell/concession considerations:

  • What is your time frame?
  • Are you trying to sell to someone who has the experience and personnel to take over the business?
  • Will it be a “turn-key” transaction?
  • How much equity do you have?
  • What is your existing debt?
  • Do you have tax liabilities?
  • What is the price?
  • What are your terms?

This is the time to make sure you have your estate planning in order!
